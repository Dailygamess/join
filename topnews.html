Unforgettable Legacy

Football’s history is written with moments that transcend the game and become part of our cultural identity. For Chile, the Copa América titles of 2015 and 2016 represent the pinnacle of our footballing history—two milestones that forever changed how the world sees Chilean football. These back-to-back victories, led by an exceptional generation, proved that a winning mindset and teamwork can overcome any barrier.

Beyond our borders, football has gifted us universal moments that remain in collective memory: Maradona’s “Goal of the Century” in Mexico ’86, Zidane’s impossible volley in the Champions League final, Germany’s historic 7-1 over Brazil in their own World Cup, or Leicester City’s miracle Premier League win against all odds. These magical instants remind us why we love this sport: its ability to surprise, excite, and unite millions worldwide under a shared passion for the ball.

At Futbol-CL-Pasion, we believe these historic moments are a source of inspiration and learning. Understanding our past helps us put the present in context and envision the future of football we want. That’s why we dedicate this section to preserving the memory of those plays, matches, and feats that defined eras and deserve to be remembered by new generations of fans.
