About Us

About Us

Futbol-CL-Pasion was born in 2020 as an initiative of a group of sports journalists and football fans. Our frustration with the lack of in-depth analysis in traditional media led us to create a digital space where journalistic rigor and passion for the beautiful game come together perfectly.

During our first years, we established ourselves as an authoritative voice in Chilean football. We expanded our team by including former players, coaches, and data analysts who bring unique perspectives to our content. We formed partnerships with international media outlets to provide global sports coverage.

Today, our mission remains clear: to democratize football analysis and make it accessible to all fans. We want to educate our audience about the tactical, technical, and cultural aspects of football. We aspire to be the bridge between the everyday fan and the more complex aspects of the game.

"Football is the most beautiful sport in the world because it unites passions, cultures, and people. Our mission is to celebrate that union every day."

Our Team

Experts Passionate About Football

Our team is made up of specialized journalists, former professional football players, tactical analysts, and sports statistics experts. Each member brings a unique perspective that enriches our content, combining practical game experience with rigorous analysis and captivating storytelling that defines Futbol-CL-Pasion.

We take pride in working with contributors who have experienced football from different angles: from the pitch to the stands, from press rooms to data analysis centers. This diversity allows us to offer a comprehensive, multi-dimensional coverage of the beautiful game, always committed to objectivity and respect for all teams and fans.
