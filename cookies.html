Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Futbol-CL-Pasion uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site usage.

1. Definition of Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when visiting a website. They are used to remember your preferences and enhance your experience.

2. Types of Cookies Used

  • Essential Cookies: necessary for the basic functioning of the site.
  • Performance Cookies: help improve speed and functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: allow us to understand how the site is used.

3. Purpose of Cookies

Cookies help us personalize content, optimize performance, and understand our readers' preferences.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies come from external services like Google Analytics. These third parties may have their own cookie policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can configure your browser to block or delete cookies at any time. However, some site features may be affected.

6. Consent

By continuing to use our site, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

7. Duration

Cookies can be session cookies (deleted when closing the browser) or persistent cookies (kept for a defined period).

8. Security

We do not use cookies to collect sensitive or personal data without your explicit consent.

9. Changes to the Policy

We may modify this Cookie Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page.
